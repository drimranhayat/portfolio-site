About Me

I am Dr. Imran Hayat, currently serving as a Lecturer in Islamic Studies at the University of Education, Lahore. I hold a PhD in Islamic Studies from the University of the Punjab, where I stood first with a CGPA of 3.95. My academic path is distinguished by multiple gold and silver medals and top positions from school to university level. My research focuses on Islamic Law, Interfaith Dialogue, Social Ethics, and Comparative Theology, with published work in recognized international journals. I have taught at Aitchison College, Punjab University, and Gender Studies departments, with a passion for empowering students through both traditional scholarship and modern pedagogy.
